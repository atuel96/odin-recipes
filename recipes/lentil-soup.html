Lentil Soup

Description

Lentil soup is a hearty and nutritious soup made from lentils, vegetables, and sometimes meat or poultry. The lentils are typically simmered in a flavorful broth with onions, carrots, celery, garlic, and other herbs and spices to create a rich and savory soup that is both satisfying and comforting.

Ingredients

Here you have a list of the ingredients you'll need:

  • 1 cup dried brown or green lentils, rinsed and drained
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2-3 carrots, peeled and chopped
  • 2-3 stalks of celery, chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 4 cups vegetable or chicken broth
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 can diced tomatoes (14.5 oz)
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Preparation

In order to prepare the meal, follow these instructions:

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the onion, carrots, celery, and garlic, and cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the vegetables are softened.
  2. Add the lentils, broth, water, diced tomatoes, thyme, rosemary, bay leaves, salt, and black pepper to the pot. Stir to combine.
  3. Bring the soup to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and let it simmer for 30-40 minutes, or until the lentils are tender and the soup is thickened.
  4. Remove the bay leaves from the soup and taste for seasoning, adding more salt or pepper as needed.
  5. Serve the soup hot, garnished with chopped fresh herbs or a drizzle of olive oil, if desired.
